- Use the marks to truthfully refer to this project or specification.
- State that your software "implements" or "targets" the Observability

## You MAY NOT, without written permission

- Claim OWS conformance unless your implementation passes the current

## Conformance claims

Any conformance claim MUST cite the specification:
https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.21193962

## Why this policy exists

"OWS-Conformant" should always mean the same, verified thing across
implementations. This policy does not restrict use of the code
(Apache-2.0) or the specification text (CC-BY 4.0) under their own
licenses — it only governs use of the Alethe and OWS-Conformant names.
